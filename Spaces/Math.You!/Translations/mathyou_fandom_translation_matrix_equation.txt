**Complex (Gaussian) notation form:**
$$
\mathcal{E}_H = (\mathcal{R}_f + \mathcal{W}_x)^{3/2} + \nabla^2_{\mu,\lambda}(\mathbb{J})
$$
$$
\mathcal{L} = \lim_{t \to \infty} \frac{e^{i\pi t}}{\mathcal{E}_H} (1 + \sin(\mathcal{W}_x))
$$

**Tensor (Einstein) notation form:**
$$
E_H = (R_f + W_x)^{3/2} + g^{\mu\nu}(\nabla_\mu \nabla_\nu J)
$$
$$
L = \lim_{t \to \infty} e^{i\pi t} E_H^{-1} (1 + \sin(W_x))
$$
